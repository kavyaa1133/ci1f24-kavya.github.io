Fall Foliage

As autumn sweeps over New York, the city transforms into a canvas of warm oranges, evoking nostalgia and comfort. The vibrant colors remind me of the changing seasons back home in Antwerp, grounding me in familiarity while also encouraging me to embrace the new.

Walking through Central Park during fall, I feel a deep connection to nature and the beauty of change. Each fallen leaf tells a story of transition, echoing my journey of adaptation in this bustling city. The oranges also symbolize the warmth of community, as neighbors come together to celebrate the season, reflecting my own experiences of building connections in this diverse city.
