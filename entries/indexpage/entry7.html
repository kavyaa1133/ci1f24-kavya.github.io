New York's subway system is more than just transportation; it is the city's pulse. Each express train line, from the vibrant red of the 2, 4, and 5 to the cool blue of the A, C, and E, reflects the energy and rhythm of the city. The reds and oranges signal urgency, while the greens and blues offer brief moments of calm amidst the rush. The steel grey symbolizes the system’s reliability and strength.


Looking at these colors, I am reminded not of a rainbow but of the subway lines that define my experience in New York City—an ever-moving reminder of the city’s power and resilience.
