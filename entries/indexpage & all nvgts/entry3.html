The minimal blues and deep greens of the Hudson River represent serenity and reflection in my journey through New York. I often find myself walking along the waterfront, mesmerized by the calm waters that contrast with the city’s energetic skyline. This river holds countless memories for me—moments of introspection as I watch the sun dip below the horizon, painting the sky with shades of orange and pink.

The river’s steady flow reminds me that beyond the chaos; there is a sense of constancy. Watching the sunset over the water brings me a sense of peace, allowing me to process my emotions and navigate the complexities of my new environment.

The blue hues evoke a sense of hope, reminding me that just like the river, life flows with its ebbs and flows.
